Return and Refund Policy

Customers may return unused items within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
Items must be in their original packaging with proof of purchase. Refunds are
processed within 5-7 business days after the returned item is received and
inspected.

Warranty Policy

All products include a one-year limited warranty from the date of purchase.
The warranty covers manufacturing defects but does not cover damage caused by
misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs. To make a warranty claim, contact
support with your order number and a description of the issue.

Shipping Policy

Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the country. Express shipping
is available at checkout and takes 1-2 business days. Shipping costs are
calculated at checkout based on order weight and destination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I change my order after it has been placed?
A: Orders can be modified within 1 hour of placement by contacting support.
After this window, orders are sent to fulfillment and cannot be changed.

Q: Do you ship internationally?
A: Yes, international shipping is available to most countries. Delivery times
and costs vary by destination.

Q: How do I track my order?
A: A tracking link is emailed to you once your order ships. You can also view
tracking information from your account order history page.
